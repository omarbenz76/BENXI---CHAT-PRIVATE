# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

BENXI is a project built on the belief that privacy and freedom from surveillance are universal human rights. We are committed to maintaining a community that reflects these values — open, respectful, and free from harassment and discrimination.

We pledge to make participation in this project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

---

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Assume good faith from fellow contributors
- Accept feedback gracefully and offer it kindly
- Focus on the work and the ideas, not the person
- Acknowledge and respect differing perspectives

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or imagery
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Trolling, deliberate disruption, or bad-faith engagement
- Any behavior that would make contributors feel unwelcome

---

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and investigated, and will result in a response appropriate to the circumstances.

Maintainers are obligated to maintain confidentiality regarding the reporter of an incident.

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, mailing lists, and any other forums created by the project team.
